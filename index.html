ABOUT

Hi, we're Digi Developers. We are multi-talented humans with over 2 years of experience in Front-End Development.

As a freelancers, our main experience is in front-end development. We have worked on a multitude of web and print based projects for a range of clients providing Web Design (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign) and Development (React Js, HTML, CSS, JS, jQuery, WordPress, Bootstrap, Responsive Layouts) and Graphic Design.


We don't like to define ourselves by the work we've done. We define ourselves by the work we want to do. Skills can be taught, personality is inherent. We prefer to keep learning, continue challenging ourselves, and do interesting things that matter.

We are currently working in Pakistan. We are always available to discuss your project via Email.
